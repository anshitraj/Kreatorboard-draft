# Kreatorboard

Kreatorboard is a modern influencer discovery and collaboration platform that connects brands with micro-influencers and creators.  
The platform enables startups and companies to discover niche creators while helping influencers manage their profiles, collaborations, and visibility.

Live Demo  
https://kreatorboard.com

---

## Overview

Kreatorboard is designed to solve the problem of discovering authentic creators in local and niche communities.

Instead of relying only on large influencers, brands can:

• Discover micro-influencers  
• Filter creators by category, location, and audience  
• Initiate collaborations directly  
• Analyze engagement metrics  

Creators can:

• Build a public creator profile  
• Showcase their work and collaborations  
• Manage brand proposals  
• Increase visibility to potential partners  

---

## Features

### Creator Profiles
Creators can build public profiles including:

- Social media links
- Audience metrics
- Collaboration history
- Portfolio content

### Brand Discovery
Brands can:

- Search influencers by niche
- Filter by engagement and category
- Identify local creators
- Contact influencers directly

### Collaboration System
The platform enables direct brand-creator collaboration with:

- Proposal management
- Campaign discussions
- Partnership tracking

### Creator Visibility
Influencers can increase exposure through:

- Verified profiles
- Category ranking
- Featured listings
